About CarePath AI

CarePath AI is developed to bridge the informational gap encountered by patients researching common symptoms online. By substituting generic search results with localized structured categorization systems (Low, Medium, and High risk profiles), patients understand standard healthcare navigation procedures immediately.

💡 Note on AI: This software architecture uses Google Gemini-2.5-Flash processing models. Medical assertions do not serve as alternative clinical checkups.

🗺️ Data & Maps: OpenStreetMap mapping frameworks operate independent of personal tracker configurations.
